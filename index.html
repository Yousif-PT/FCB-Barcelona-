Timeline

2024-25

Under Hansi Flick’s first year, FCB made a strong comeback to dominance, winning La Liga, Copa del Rey, and the Supercopa de España in the 2024-25 season. His high-pressing and attacking philosophy helped unlock the potential of Barcelona’s young talent

2014-15

one of the greatest in football history. Guided by Luis Enrique and powered by the iconic MSN trio (Messi, Suárez, Neymar), the team achieved a historic treble — winning La Liga, Copa del Rey, and the Champions League.

2008-09

FC Barcelona won six trophies (the Sextuple) in 2009, becoming the first European club to achieve this historic feat. La Liga, Copa del Rey, Supercopa de España, UEFA Champions League, UEFA Super Cup, FIFA Club World Cup

2005-06

season represents the peak of the Ronaldinho era, a magical time that restored Barcelona to the top of European football and opened the door for the next generation, including Lionel Messi.
